Scottish Terrier

The Scottish Terrier, or Scottie dog, is a small and sturdy breed renowned for its distinctive appearance, including a wiry coat and bushy eyebrows.

Known for their bravery and loyalty, Scotties make excellent watchdogs and loving companions, forming strong bonds with their families.

West Highland White Terrier

The West Highland White Terrier, often affectionately called the Westie, is a charming and energetic small breed with a distinctive white coat that gives it a delightful appearance.

Known for their friendly and outgoing nature, Westies are playful and confident companions, making them popular family pets and excellent watchdogs due to their alert and lively personality.

Norwich Terrier

The Norwich Terrier, characterized by its affectionate and fearless nature, is a compact breed with a slightly longer body and a distinct wiry coat.

Known for their playful spirit and strong bond with their families, making them excellent pets for those seeking a loyal and spirited canine companion.
